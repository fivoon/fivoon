Privacy Policy

Last updated on 17 July 2025

Welcome to Fivoon ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and related services.


1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details when you voluntarily provide it to us — for example, when you subscribe to updates, schedule a call, or contact us directly through the website.

1.2 Usage Information

We may automatically collect technical data about your interaction with our website, including your IP address, browser type, device details, and pages visited. This helps us understand visitor behavior and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Operating and maintaining our website

  • Communicating with you about your account, inquiries, or services

  • Sending newsletters, updates, and promotional content

  • Analyzing trends to improve our offerings

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However, we may share your data with trusted third-party service providers that assist us in website operation, marketing, or analytics—only to the extent necessary to perform their functions.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and related technologies to improve your browsing experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect website functionality.

5. Your Choices

You can unsubscribe from our communications or request to update/delete your data at any time by contacting us. We respect your preferences and privacy.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page. Your continued use of our website after changes are made constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
